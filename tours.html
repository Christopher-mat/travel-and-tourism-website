SAFARI TOURS

POPULAR

  • Okavango Delta:

    Known for its unique inland delta ecosystem, it's a haven for wildlife, including the "Big Five".
    Activities include mokoro safaris (traditional canoe trips) and game drives.

  • Chobe National Park:

    Famous for its large elephant population and its proximity to the Chobe River. Activities include game drives, boat cruises, and birdwatching.

  • Kalahari Desert:

    Offers a unique experience with its semi-arid landscapes and diverse wildlife, including the desert-adapted animals.

    CULTURE TOURS

    • Village Visits:

      Visit villages like Manyana and Molepolole, located within a 50km radius of Gaborone, for a glimpse into rural life and traditions.

      • Heritage Adventures:

      This company offers a range of tours, including a history and culture tour, a literary tour based on the No. 1 Ladies Detective Agency books, and bespoke trips tailored to your interests.

      Art and Craft Tour:

      Visit local artisans and craft workshops to learn about traditional skills like weaving and pottery.

    • LUXURY TOURS

      • Fly-in Safaris:

      Fly-in safaris provide direct access to remote locations, often featuring private lodges and camps.

      • Mobile Safaris:

      These offer a more adventurous experience, with accommodation in luxury tents and a focus on wildlife encounters in the bush.
